Dr. Farouk Elkassed

Dr. Farouk is well-known for being an excellent therapist with unique skills in manual therapy.

His approach is to look at the body as a whole. He takes into account the physical, emotional, psychological, and social qualities of the individual, treating these elements to better achieve the patient's goals.

He treats patients from 5 to 90 years old, with acute and chronic conditions.
