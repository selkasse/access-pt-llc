Dr. Farouk is well known for being an excellent therapist with unique skills of manual therapy.

His approach is to look at the body as a whole, including the:

  • physical,
  • emotional,
  • psychological, and
  • social components

of the individual, treating these elements to better achieve the patients’ goals. He treats patients from 5 to 90 years old with acute and chronic conditions.

His patients receive:

  • One hour comprehensive evaluation.
  • Thirty minutes of hands-on treatment for their following visits.
  • Consistent treatment because Dr. Farouk is a solo practitioner.
